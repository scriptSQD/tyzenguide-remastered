What is Tyzenguide project about?

Antoni Tyzenhauz is one of the most prominent figures in the history of our wonderful city. Thanks to his activities the architectural character of Hrodna was changed and a number of marvellous historical sights appeared. Antoni Tyzenhauz was a reformer, a dreamer, an enthusiast, gushing with ideas and actions. He inspired and was inspired. In turn, we decided to walk along the memorable roads of Tyzenhauz in Hrodna to show his priceless heritage. We hope you'll enjoy the guide to the sights of our heartwarming city commemorating the personality of Antoni Tyzenhauz and his outstanding deeds.
